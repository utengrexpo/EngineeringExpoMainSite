SENIOR RECEPTION

Senior Reception will be held in person at The University of Texas At Austin Club on Tuesday, September 5 from 6 PM to 9 PM.

Senior Reception is an exclusive networking event that allows seniors seeking full-time positions to interact with recruiters in a casual setting the night before Expo.

Recruiters:

We invite representatives of employers to join us at this exclusive networking night to meet current engineering seniors the night before Expo. Hors d’oeuvres and refreshments will be provided. This will be a fantastic opportunity to meet a variety of full-time candidates and learn about their personality and qualities beyond their resume in a casual setting.

Candidates:

All engineering seniors are invited to participate in this event to meet recruiters the night before Expo. Hors d’oeuvres and refreshments will be provided. Students are encouraged to leave resumes at home and use this opportunity to network with employers in a casual setting. Students are not required to stay for the whole event and can come and go as they please.

Come out to Senior Reception and get networking!
