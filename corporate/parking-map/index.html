Map and Parking


Frank Erwin Center

PARKING

Recruiters should check in on the south side loading dock of the Frank Erwin Center, via the service entrance on the Interstate 35 southbound frontage road.

Locations
There will be primary parking in Lot 108, immediately south of the Erwin Center. There will also be overflow parking in the Trinity Garage (TRG) or Health Center Garage (HCG).
TRG is at the intersection of Martin Luther King Boulevard and Trinity Street, and HCG is at the intersection of E 15th street and Trinity Street.

The loading dock is to be used for loading/unloading of display materials only. We strongly suggest carpooling to the arena.

Parking Validation
Parking will be validated. Recruiters will be able to pick up a permit before they leave the event and use it to get out of the garage for free.
