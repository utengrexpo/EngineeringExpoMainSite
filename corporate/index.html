This year's Expo will take place at the University of Texas at Austin's RecSports Center on Tuesday, September 10 and Wednesday, September 11. The RecSports Center is located at 2001 San Jacinto Blvd.  (map image: Map data Â©2022 Google)



The UT Austin Fall Engineering Expo, hosted by the Student Engineering Council, is one of the largest student-run career fairs in the nation. This two-day event attracts more than 300 employers and 7000 candidates each year.

Expo provides a unique, interactive opportunity to meet and connect with some of the most talented engineers in the nation from the Cockrell School of Engineering. For more than 30 years, Expo has brought together employers and candidates to build lasting careers. When it comes to expanding your organization and remaining competitive in the industry, Expo is the perfect opportunity!
